The next stage of this experiment is a betting game. Before the start of the game, you will go through a quiz asking questions about these instructions. You will earn yourself 500 points for the quiz - however, for every question that you answer incorrectly, 100 points will be deducted. You can see the result of your quiz during the summary at the end of the experiment.

In the game, you will start with 600 points. You can then decide whether you want to bet some of your points. There is no minimum bet (which means you can bet 0 points and play without stakes). The maximum bet that you can place is 30 points.

The game itself will be similar to a one-armed bandit. You will see 2 windows with 6 different pictures flashing through. You will be able to stop the left of the images by pressing a button labeled "STOP". However, this button does not work very precisely and the stop could be delayed slightly. Note that the second image will stop randomly.

You win the game if the left picture shows a shamrock. If you lose the game, you will lose your bet. If you win the game, you will be rewarded with points worth your bet multiplied by a certain value, your "multiplicator". This multiplicator is 4.5 for your first game, and will decrease by 0.1 after every game. After each game, you will be asked whether you want to play another round or leave the game and continue to the next stage. If you decide to leave the game your points balance will be saved and converted into Euros at the end of the experiment.
